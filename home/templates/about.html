This website was created by three highschool seniors interning at a digital agency. Our goal is to educate users about some of the major issues that America has been facing for a while. The website shares informative information about the problems that Americans either will encounter or already encounter in their day to day lives, whether they know it or not.

Raevyn

My name is Raevyn Tabron I am a rising senior at Mckinley Tech High school. I am also a summer intern at the company I Strategy labs. ISL for abbreviation. The topic I chose and was most interested in was gentrification. Out of all the topics to choose from I picked gentrification because I see the changes happening in my community all of the time. I also wanted to learn how did people really feel towards the changes in their community, did they take a negative approach or a positive approach.One of the challenges I faced throughout creating the website was learning the basics of coding. I never was taught or felt the interest to code until I became an intern here. It was a very challenging stage but I persevered and created my own page on the website.
