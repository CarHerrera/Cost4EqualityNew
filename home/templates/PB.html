Police Brutality

Police Brutality is a form of police misconduct which involves unjust violence towards civilians. In the United States of America, there have been too many cases of harassment, unjust assault, and misuse of power from Police officers. Police brutality is important as it is still very relevant and is still happening to this day. The fact that the people who are being employed to protect and serve the civilians, but have the ability to kill unarmed civilians and get off without facing any consequences is purely despicable.

Issue vs. Solution

The issue is police officers have the license to kill and abuse their power on a daily basis. Most people in society (majority that are minorities) are afraid to even be in the presence of an officer. Innocent citizen should not have to fear for their lives from the people who are supposed to protect them.One solution is to have more training for officers. Study shows that it takes longer to become a hair stylist than an officer. Doing someone hair and protecting the people are on two very different levels of importance. We need to make sure that officers have a sufficient amount of training to be able to assess situations accordingly without having to make tragically hasty decisions. It is unfortunate that parents have to sit down and warn their child about the dangers of being in a situation when dealing with an officer. Parents have to educate their child on their rights and how to use the appropriate protocol. Another solution is that officers and citizens of different communities should all be attending community events. This will help establish trust between communities and the officers which is a very important aspect towards building a better community and relationship with a police officer.

We as a society are far from perfect but our justice system and our police departments fundamentally need to improve for the sake of the future of America.
